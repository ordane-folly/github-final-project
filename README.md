# Simple Interest Calculator

A simple interest calculator that helps you compute interest on a principal amount.

## Formula
Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100

## Variables
- **Principal (P)**: The initial amount of money
- **Rate (R)**: The annual interest rate (as a percentage)
- **Time (T)**: The time period in years

## How to Use
1. Enter the principal amount
2. Enter the annual interest rate
3. Enter the time period in years
4. The calculator will compute the simple interest

## Example
If Principal = 1000, Rate = 5%, Time = 2 years
Simple Interest = (1000 × 5 × 2) / 100 = 100

## Implementation
This calculator can be implemented in various programming languages including Bash, Python, JavaScript, etc.
